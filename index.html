01

Quote & payment

Customer contacts Pivot to request a quote. A rep builds it in HubSpot and sends a payment request. Customer pays by credit card, ACH, or PO/invoice. Learning access is not blocked by payment status — but scheduler access is gated until payment is confirmed.

Select a section:
Customer contacts Pivot→Rep reviews seat needs→Quote built in HubSpot→Quote sent to customer

What the customer does

  • Reaches out to Pivot (email, web form, or existing contact)
  • Provides org name, number of staff, and desired certification tier(s)
  • Indicates payment method preference (CC, ACH, or PO)
  • Reviews quote and confirms

What Pivot does

  • Rep creates or locates the Company record in HubSpot
  • Confirms seat counts and license mix (Associate / Practitioner / Instructor)
  • Applies correct volume tier pricing automatically
  • Generates quote and sends via HubSpot

  • Company record created or updated
  • Subscription type, seat count, and license mix recorded
  • Quote attached to the deal
  • Payment method preference noted
Customer receives payment link→Pays via Authorize.net→Payment status → HubSpot→QuickBooks sync

What the customer does

  • Receives payment link generated from HubSpot
  • Completes credit card or ACH payment via Authorize.net
  • Receives payment confirmation automatically

What happens in the system

  • Authorize.net processes the transaction
  • Payment confirmed status pushed back to HubSpot
  • HubSpot syncs invoice and payment record to QuickBooks (one-way)
  • Subscription active flag set — triggers learning access
  • Payment confirmed flag set — unlocks scheduler access for Practitioner and Instructor tiers

Customer submits PO→Rep records in HubSpot→Invoice generated→PO payment confirmed via HubSpot workflow

What the customer does

  • Submits purchase order via their procurement process
  • Provides PO number and billing contact to Pivot rep
  • Receives HubSpot-generated invoice against the PO
  • Remits payment by check or ACH per invoice terms

What Pivot does

  • Rep records PO number in HubSpot deal record
  • HubSpot generates and sends invoice — syncs to QuickBooks
  • HubSpot workflow automatically marks payment confirmed when payment is received and reconciled
  • Overdue accounts: automated lockout triggered by HubSpot workflow per defined thresholds
HubSpot — PO + invoice workflowQuickBooks — accounting

Access note: Subscription active = learning access on immediately for PO customers. Scheduler access for institutional admin(s) is unlocked automatically once payment is confirmed via HubSpot workflow. No partial payments. Refunds supported.

Subscription active flag (HubSpot)

  • Set when subscription start date is entered by rep
  • Drives: Admin access to Catalog seat management
  • Drives: Learner enrollment and Canvas Career access
  • Active as long as subscription is current
  • Does NOT require payment confirmation to be set

Payment confirmed flag (HubSpot)

  • Set automatically when CC/ACH payment clears via Authorize.net
  • Set automatically via HubSpot workflow when PO/invoice payment is received and reconciled
  • Drives: Scheduler access for institutional admin(s) with Instructor subscriptions
  • Overdue accounts: automated lockout triggered by HubSpot workflow per defined thresholds
  • This is the only payment gate in the system

Key principle

  • Learning access is NOT tied to payment status — only to subscription being active
  • Scheduler access IS tied to payment confirmation — available to institutional admin(s) with Instructor subscriptions; automated once payment confirmed flag is set
  • A PO customer can start learning immediately — they just can't schedule the live training until payment clears

Access & enrollment

Once payment is confirmed and the subscription is active, a CSM kickoff call is scheduled and the training agreement is signed. The institutional admin(s) then gain access to Catalog for seat management. Assigning a seat in Catalog immediately grants that learner access to their Canvas Career curriculum — no additional step required.

Payment confirmed→Training agreement signed→CSM kickoff call scheduled→Institutional admin(s) onboarded

Training agreement

  • Institutional admin(s) review and sign the Pivot training agreement before access is provisioned
  • Agreement covers: scope of certification, permitted use of materials, compliance obligations, and recertification requirements
  • Signed agreement recorded in HubSpot on the Company record
  • Access to Catalog and Canvas is provisioned after agreement is signed

CSM kickoff call

  • CSM schedules a kickoff call with the institutional admin(s) after agreement is signed
  • Kickoff covers: platform walkthrough, seat assignment process, skills lab scheduling overview, and key contacts
  • CSM confirms the admin understands their responsibilities before the cohort is launched
  • Any Instructor-tier subscribers are briefed on the Host a Training / Join a Training paths and training agreement for hosting

Seat assignment process

Seat reallocation policy

Scheduling the skills lab

After payment is confirmed, institutional admin(s) with an Instructor subscription gain access to the scheduling tool. Associates and Practitioners do not perform any scheduling — their participation is arranged by the institutional admin(s). Two paths: host a training at your own location, or join an existing nearby training.

Select a section:

Access gate: Scheduling is only accessible to institutional admin(s) with an Instructor tier in their subscription. Associates and Practitioners do not perform any scheduling activities — their participation in the skills lab is arranged by the institutional admin(s) on their behalf.

What triggers scheduler access

  • For CC / ACH: Authorize.net confirms payment → HubSpot automatically sets payment confirmed flag
  • For PO / invoice: HubSpot workflow triggers payment confirmed flag automatically on receipt of payment
  • Account lockout for overdue accounts is automated — access suspended when payment is overdue per defined thresholds
  • Once payment confirmed flag is set, institutional admin(s) can access the scheduling tool

Two scheduling paths available

  • Path A — Host a Training: Institutional admin(s) provide the venue, choose a 3-day window, and optionally open the event to nearby orgs
  • Path B — Join a Training: Institutional admin(s) browse already-scheduled nearby trainings and register their staff into available seats
  • Both paths use the same Training Event object in HubSpot
  • Associates and Practitioners are registered by the institutional admin(s) — they do not access the scheduler directly
Admin selects "Host a Training"→Chooses 3-day window→Trainer availability checked→Event created in HubSpot→Synced to Google Calendar→Opened to nearby orgs

What the institutional admin(s) do

  • Accesses scheduling tool (payment confirmed gate cleared)
  • Signs the hosting training agreement before the event is confirmed
  • Selects "Host a Training" and provides venue details
  • Views calendar showing valid trainer availability windows
  • Selects preferred 3-day window from available options
  • Registers all attendees (Associates, Practitioners, and Instructors) into the event in HubSpot — Catalog is not used for event scheduling
  • Can request the event be marked private if at least 10 people are registered — private events are not visible to nearby institutions

What happens in the system

  • Custom API validates trainer availability against Google Calendar
  • Training Event object created in HubSpot with all details
  • Event synced to Google Calendar for the assigned trainer
  • Event marked as "Open / Shared" by default — or "Private" if requested and 10+ registrants are confirmed
  • Capacity tracked in HubSpot — no Catalog involvement in event scheduling
HubSpot — Training Event objectGoogle Calendar — trainer schedulingCustom API — availability validation
Admin selects "Join a Training"→Browses nearby events→Selects event→Assigns staff to seats→Recorded in HubSpot

What the institutional admin(s) do

  • Accesses scheduling tool (payment confirmed gate cleared)
  • Browses calendar or list of open nearby training events (private events are not visible)
  • Filters by geography and available dates
  • Registers all attending staff (Associates, Practitioners, and Instructors) into the event in HubSpot

What happens in the system

  • Seat assignments recorded in HubSpot and tied to the specific Training Event
  • Institution added to the participating institutions list on the event
  • Available seat count decremented
  • Learners linked to the event in HubSpot for completion tracking
HubSpot — Training Event object + attendee registration

Training Event object (HubSpot)

  • Event type: Hosted or Open/Shared or Private (if 10+ registered and privacy requested)
  • Host institution and location
  • Hosting training agreement signed — recorded on event
  • Assigned trainer — 3-day training window
  • Total capacity and registered attendees
  • Participating institutions
  • Nearby/open or private availability status
  • Event status (Scheduled / In Progress / Complete)

System roles

  • HubSpot: source of truth for all event records, seat assignments, and status
  • Google Calendar: trainer availability engine and calendar execution
  • Custom APIs: validate availability, create events, sync between HubSpot and Google Calendar
  • Catalog: seat management — not event scheduling

Training mats — institution responsibility

  • Institutions hosting a training must purchase their own training mats before the event
  • Pivot provides a list of preferred vendors — institutions source and purchase independently
  • Mats are not provided or shipped by Pivot
  • Confirm mat requirements with Pivot rep when scheduling

Curriculum manual — Instructors

  • Each Instructor receives a physical copy and a digital copy of the Pivot curriculum manual
  • Physical copy is shipped to the onsite training location and distributed to Instructors during the skills lab
  • Physical manuals are also available for purchase for those who need additional or replacement copies
  • Digital copy accessible within Canvas Career at any time
  • Associates and Practitioners receive digital access in Canvas only — no physical manual
  • Manual is licensed to the individual — not to be copied or distributed

All other materials — Canvas + print

  • All supporting materials made available within Canvas Career
  • Accessible at any time during the active subscription year
  • Materials can be downloaded and printed if desired
  • Digital access covers all tiers: Associate, Practitioner, and Instructor

Materials summary by tier

  • Associate: Digital materials in Canvas (printable)
  • Practitioner: Digital materials in Canvas (printable)
  • Instructor: Physical curriculum manual (shipped to onsite training location, distributed at skills lab; also available for purchase) + digital manual in Canvas + all other digital materials in Canvas (printable)
  • Hosting institution: Must procure training mats from Pivot-preferred vendors

Certification record & recertification

All certifications are tied to the calendar year — not individual expiration dates. Every learner must recertify once per calendar year regardless of when they originally certified. Recertification is included in the subscription. Learners complete recertification modules in Canvas; Practitioners and Instructors also attend a skills lab. Specialization upgrades are requested here and assessed at the recertification skills lab.

  • Badgr Pro credential ID referenced
  • Any upgrade/specialization history

  • Learner owns their Badgr profile — persists independently of employer

Important: Pivot does not use individual certification expiration dates. All learners must recertify once per calendar year — the recertification window is the same for everyone, regardless of when they originally certified.

Calendar year recertification model

  • All certifications are valid for the current calendar year
  • Every learner must recertify once each calendar year to maintain active status
  • No individual expiration dates — the cadence is universal across all learners and institutions
  • Recertification is included in the annual subscription — no additional cost
  • Simplifies compliance tracking for admins — one deadline, everyone in the institution

Admin visibility

  • Looker Studio dashboard shows recertification status for the current calendar year at any time
  • Admin can check how many learners have recertified vs outstanding at will — no scheduled report required
  • HubSpot tracks current year certification status per learner
  • Admin notified automatically when each recertification credential is issued

Upgrades are included in the subscription. Learners who want to add a specialization do not pay extra. Upgrades are not available on-demand — they align with the annual recertification cycle and are assessed at the recertification skills lab.

What training upgrades are

  • Additional specializations added on top of a learner's current certification tier
  • Expand the scope of what a certified learner is authorized to practice or teach
  • Included in the subscription — no separate purchase or upgrade fee
  • Available to all active certified learners in good standing
  • Processed at the annual recertification cycle — not available mid-year on demand

How to request an upgrade

  • Admin or learner contacts their Pivot rep to request a specialization upgrade
  • Request should be submitted ahead of the scheduled recertification skills lab
  • Pivot rep confirms the specialization is available and included in the subscription
  • Upgrade is added to the learner's upcoming recertification plan
  • Additional online modules for the specialization are assigned in Canvas prior to the skills lab

During the recertification skills lab

  • Learner completes standard recertification requirements as normal
  • Upgrade specialization is assessed as an additional component within the same skills lab
  • Instructor uses the specialization checklist (PDF in Canvas — same interim process)
  • Pass/fail recorded for both standard recertification and the upgrade component separately
  • Instructor marks the gradebook in Canvas for both components

Timing & eligibility

  • Upgrades are not processed mid-year — they align with the annual recertification window
  • Learner must hold an active certification in the current year to be eligible
  • Cannot upgrade a lapsed certification
  • Learner must complete all assigned upgrade modules in Canvas before the skills lab
  • Planning ahead ensures the trainer and materials are prepared

Policies & administration

Institutions have on-demand access to their Looker Studio dashboard to view certification status, seat utilization, and learner progress at any time. Admins are notified automatically when credentials are issued. The policies below govern subscriptions, certifications, and admin responsibilities throughout the year.

Select a section:
Institution created in HubSpot→Unique institution ID assigned→Looker Studio dashboard auto-provisioned→Admin receives access

How the dashboard is provisioned

  • When a new institution is created in HubSpot, a unique Company ID is assigned
  • This triggers an automation that filters the standard Looker Studio template for that institution
  • One standardized template — dynamically filtered per institution ID
  • No custom reports built per customer
  • Admin receives dashboard access link (shared link or email-based access control)

What the dashboard shows

  • Learner progress: active vs inactive, completion % by course
  • Completion rates across the institution
  • Certification status by learner and tier
  • Recertification status for the current calendar year
  • Seat utilization: assigned vs unassigned seats by tier
  • Specialization / upgrade history per learner

On-demand access — check anytime

  • Dashboard is available at any time — admins do not need to wait for a scheduled report
  • Admin is notified automatically by Badgr Pro each time a credential is issued for one of their learners
  • HubSpot is the primary data source — data is always current
  • Each admin sees only their institution's data, filtered by institution ID
  • Multiple admins can be designated per institution in HubSpot
  • Dashboard access is removed when the subscription lapses
HubSpot — primary data sourceLooker Studio — on-demand dashboardBadgr Pro — credential issuance notifications to adminCanvas Career — completion data

Subscription & access policies

  • Learning access active for all assigned learners while subscription is current
  • Overdue accounts: automated lockout triggered by HubSpot workflow per defined thresholds
  • Scheduler access requires payment confirmed flag — available to institutional admin(s) with Instructor subscriptions; unlocked automatically
  • No partial payments supported. Refunds supported.
  • Seats can be reassigned — up to 20% of total licenses may be reallocated per subscription year
  • Reallocation beyond 20% requires a conversation with the Pivot rep
  • Training upgrades / specializations included in subscription — no additional cost

Certification policies

  • All learners must recertify once per calendar year — no individual expiration dates
  • Recertification is included in the subscription — no additional purchase
  • Annual content refresh content is part of recertification modules in Canvas
  • Lapsed certifications (not recertified within the calendar year) flagged in HubSpot and visible in Looker Studio
  • Lapsed credential holders cannot represent themselves as currently certified
  • Upgrades must be requested ahead of the recertification skills lab — not available on demand mid-year

Admin responsibilities

  • Assign all purchased seats within the subscription period
  • Manage seat reallocations within the 20% annual allowance
  • Submit upgrade requests to Pivot rep ahead of the recertification skills lab
  • Procure training mats from Pivot-preferred vendors before hosting a training
  • Coordinate scheduling for skills lab (Practitioner and Instructor tiers)
  • Monitor learner progress via the Looker Studio dashboard as needed
  • Notify Pivot of seat reassignments beyond the 20% threshold

Pivot's ongoing responsibilities

  • CSM monitors account health and reaches out proactively
  • Automated notifications sent to admins on each credential issuance
  • Annual content refresh published and pushed to active subscriptions
  • Physical curriculum manuals shipped to newly certified Instructors
  • HubSpot and Looker Studio kept current — data is always accurate
  • Preferred vendor list maintained and shared for training mat procurement
